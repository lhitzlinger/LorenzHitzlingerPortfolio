Ghost Recorder: A Motion Capture System for VR Headsets

Developed @MAN Energy Solutions (Everllence)

Project Overview

  • Engine: Unity
  • Programming Language: C#
  • Used Hardware: Meta Quest 3
  • Team Size: 2
  • Status: finished

Description

During my working student position, I developed, together with another working student, a VR motion capture system that allows individual work steps to be recorded for VR training. This is intended to serve as an interactive tutorial, enabling new employees to see directly on the models in their VR training what they need to do.

My Tasks

  • Implementation of the System
  • Recording of Tasks for a specific Training
  • Integrating different types of interactable objects in the system
