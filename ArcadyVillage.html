Arcady Village

Your own Arcade Hall Adventure

Project Overview

  • Engine: Unity
  • Programming Language: C#
  • Team Size: 2
  • Status: In Development
  • Since: 10/2022

Descritpion

Arcady Village is a cozy game about managing your own arcade hall. Get to know the residents of Arcady Village and beat their high scores in all the playable arcade games. The game is comparable to farming simulations like Stardew Valley or Animal Crossing, but instead of the farming aspect, you manage your arcade hall and beat high scores in arcade games.

My Tasks

  • Implementation of the NPC System (Master's Thesis) (HTN/GOAP)
  • Development of all kinds of Gameplay Systems and Arcade Games
    (Save & Load, Player Movement, Placing Arcade Machines ...)
  • 3D Modelling of all kinds of Objects with Blender
  • Creating Humanoid Animations with Cascadeur
  • All kinds of 2D sprites for Arcade Games (Aseprite, Inkscape, Drawing Tablet)
