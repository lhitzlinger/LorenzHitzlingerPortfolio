About me

I began my Bachelor’s degree in Game Engineering in the winter semester of 2018 at Kempten University of Applied Sciences and completed it successfully in 2022. Immediately afterward, I started my Master’s degree in Game Engineering and Visual Computing, also at Kempten University of Applied Sciences, which I completed with excellent results in 2025.

During my Bachelor's studies, I completed a six-month internship at Immersight GmbH, where I gained in-depth experience in Wondows software development using Qt and C++. During my Master's studies, I worked for 2.5 years as a working student at MAN Energy Solutions (now Everllence). My tasks there mainly involved developing VR visualizations and programming new features as well as improving existing ones.

I place high value on clean and well-readable code and am always interested in improving my workflow in creating software constructs.
